Welcome to Ozamiz City

Ozamiz, officially the City of Ozamiz, is a 3rd class component city in the province of Misamis Occidental, Philippines. According to the 2020 census, it has a population of 140,334 people making it the most populous city in Misamis Occidental.

Mission

create a progressive and harmonious community in Ozamiz City by promoting sustainable development, preserving cultural and historical heritage, and ensuring the well-being of its residents through inclusive and transparent governance.

Vission

Ozamiz City aspires to be a model of economic growth, cultural vibrancy, and environmental stewardship, where residents and visitors thrive in a safe, prosperous, and sustainable urban environment.
