Top 3 Tour Spots OF Ozamiz

1.Rama Beach

Rama Beach in Clarin (Misamis Occidental) is a very dirty public beach and it is not maintained at all for the environment. The sea is brown and the sand is black, there are only a few old bungalows to live in, in case of bad weather.

But you can't eat there because there are a lot of dogs and everything is abandoned. Also, the employees of the city who stay at the entrance of the beach charged us quite a lot, because we sat in one of those so-called old huts: 250 pesos for a couple and for 1 hour of relaxation.

2.Hoyohoy Highland Stone Chapel Adventure Park

Hoyohoy Highland Stone Chapel Adventure Park in Tangub City offers stunning views, thrilling activities, and a serene stone chapel, making it perfect for adventure and relaxation.

3.Pidua-an Falls

This 60-meter curtain-like falls is located in Sitio Piduan, Barangay Napangan, Don Victoriano, Misamis Occidental. Nestled in the heart of the Malindang Mountain Range, Piduan Falls is a mystical experience with crystal clear waters that is cool and even safe to drink.

The 4-kilometer trek to the falls takes more or less 2 hours of walking among the untouched beauty and serenity that is typical of the Misamis Occidental forests.
